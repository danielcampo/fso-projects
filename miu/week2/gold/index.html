Do you collect games?

With Game Collector you'll be able to keep track of your entire game collection all in one, easy-to-use interface.

Purpose

Game Collector is essentially an inventory keeping application that is strictly tailored for games. Categorize your games by genre, publisher, developer and your favorites.

As Game Collector is further developed you'll be able to gain an overview of your entire collection including the value of your collection, your favorite genre and your favorite publishers and developers.
